1. Characteristics
    • able to organize people, time, and resources for Christian service
    • able to make effective plans to accomplish goals
    • able to delegate important tasks to the proper person at the proper time
    • able to perform effective follow-up

    • tendency to rely on well-made plans and upon yourself instead of the Holy Spirit

    • a business attitude may leave very little room for other people's ideas, faith, and creativity
